list-style-position:outside (default)

  • Coffee - A brewed drink prepared from roasted coffee beans, which are seeds of the berries from the Coffea plant.
  • Tea - An aromatic beverage commonly prepared by pouring hot or boiling water over cured leaves of the Camellia sinensis, an evergreen shrub(bush) native to Asia.
  • Coca Cola - A carbonated soft drink produced by The Coca-Cola Company. The drink's name refers to of its original ingredients, which were kola nuts (a source of caffeine) and coca leaves.
